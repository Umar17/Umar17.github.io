Introduction

I am a speech processing researcher and currently working as a Research Officer at Center for Language Engineering (CLE) at University of Engineering and Technology (UET), Lahore.


I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. Electrical Engineering degrees from UET, Lahore in 2017 and 2019 respectively. I joined CLE during my senior year as speech processing researcher to develop a Large Vocabulary Continuous Speech Recognition (LVCSR) system for Urdu language, headed by Dr. Sarmad Hussain.
